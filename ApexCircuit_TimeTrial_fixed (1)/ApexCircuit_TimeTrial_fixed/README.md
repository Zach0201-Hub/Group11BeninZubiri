# APEX CIRCUIT – TIME TRIAL

## What's New (Time Trial Edition)
- **Solo time trial** – no AI opponents; it's just you vs the clock.
- **Lap count selection** – choose 3, 5, or 10 laps from the menu before each session.
- **Ghost car** – after your first lap, a translucent cyan ghost replays your best lap. Race against yourself.
- **4 Sector timing** – the track is split into 4 sectors. After each sector you see a colour-coded delta:
  - 🟢 Green = faster than your best lap's sector
  - 🔴 Red = slower
  - 🟣 Purple = no reference yet (first lap)
- **Full results screen** – per-lap times, best lap highlighted (◆), delta vs best, average, total time, and sector breakdown.
- **Arcade timer removed** – race ends naturally when all laps are complete.

## Controls
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| ↑ / W | Accelerate |
| ↓ / S | Brake |
| ← / A | Steer left |
| → / D | Steer right |
| ESC | Pause |

## How to Run
**Windows:** Double-click `run_windows.bat`
**macOS/Linux:** `chmod +x run_mac_linux.sh && ./run_mac_linux.sh`
